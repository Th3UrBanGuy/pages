Streamlining
Visitor Appointments

An automated, high-performance scheduling architecture designed to eliminate administrative inefficiencies at BGC Trust University Bangladesh.

Read Paper View Cert

The Problem

Legacy paper-based visitor logs caused data loss, security risks, and long physical queues at the administrative offices.

The Solution

A full-stack web application implementing automated slot allocation, admin dashboards, and secure visitor authentication.

The Impact

Drastically reduced wait times and digitized the entire visitor entry workflow for better data retention.
